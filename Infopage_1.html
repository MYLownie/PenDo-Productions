Overview

PenDo Academy follows step-siblings Kia and Phoenix as they ascend military ranks and fight to become worthy successors of their late parents. For 76 years, the region of Sem Vida has been decimated by war between humans and demons, and many lives were lost during the last great ambush; The Siege of Sem Vida that rendered thousands, including Phoenix and Kia, orphans.

Choni, Jean, Phoenix and Kia believe that they have what it takes to bring this era of war to an end....
